# Dashboards

Dashboards are the heart of InsightEngine. They allow you to visualize your data and gain insights at a glance.

## Creating a Dashboard

1. Navigate to the **Dashboards** tab.
2. Click **Create New Dashboard**.
3. Give your dashboard a name and description.

## Adding Cards

Dashboard cards are individual visualizations.

- Click **Add Card**.
- Select a data source.
- Build your query using the Visual Query Builder.
- Choose a chart type (Bar, Line, Pie, etc.).

## Sharing

You can share dashboards with other users or generate a public link.

- Click the **Share** button in the top right.
- Enter email addresses or toggle "Public Link".
